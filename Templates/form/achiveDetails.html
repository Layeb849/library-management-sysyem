Project Overview

Our organization successfully conducted its large-scale winter clothing distribution program this year, continuing our annual tradition of serving the community.

When the severe cold waves hit the northern regions of the country, leaving many vulnerable families in distress, our dedicated volunteer teams were on the ground to provide immediate relief. The initiative went beyond just distributing blankets. We provided sweaters, caps, and socks for children, along with warm shawls and heavy coats for the elderly.

"The warmth we shared this winter wasn't just from the clothes, but from the collective kindness of our donors."
