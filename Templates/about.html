About Central Library

A Journey of Knowledge, Growth, and Community Service Since 1985

Our Story

How It All Began

In 1985, a group of passionate educators and community leaders came together with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a space where knowledge would be freely accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or economic status. Central Library was born from this dream in the heart of Dhaka, Bangladesh.

What started as a modest collection of 500 books in a small rented space has now grown into one of the most comprehensive public libraries in the region, housing over 50,000 books, thousands of digital resources, and serving more than 2,000 members annually.

The library was established with a clear mission: to foster a love for reading, support academic excellence, and provide a welcoming environment for lifelong learning. Over the decades, we have stayed true to this mission while continuously evolving to meet the changing needs of our community.

1985
