Our Story

How It All Began

In 2023, a remarkable journey began in a remote village where access to knowledge was once very limited. What seemed unimaginable to many was turned into reality through the vision and determination of a few passionate individuals. The idea was first introduced by Rkibul, which later inspired Layeb Hossain, Rubel Hasan, and Mehedi Hasan to come together and establish Pathokbondhu Pathagar and Research Center.

The journey started with only 100 books, driven by hope and a strong commitment to serve the community. Over time, through dedication and continuous effort, the library has grown significantly and now holds a collection of 20,000+ books, becoming a vital source of knowledge and inspiration for people of all ages.

Located in a remote rural area, where such an initiative was once beyond imagination, the library stands today as a symbol of change and possibility. With a mission to promote reading, support education, and inspire the younger generation, Pathokbondhu Pathagar continues to evolve as a center for learning, research, and community development.

Meet Our Founders

The visionaries who made it all possible

Founder

Rokibul Islam

Chief Founder & Visionary

Co-Founder

Md Layeb Hossain
